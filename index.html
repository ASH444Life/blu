About Blu

Fine dining with fresh seasonal ingredients.

Blu is an elegant fine dining restaurant overlooking Lake Michigan in Glen Arbor. Its menu is newly created each day, taking advantage of fresh, seasonal, and local ingredients.

Offerings may include fresh fish, beef, veal, duck, lamb, wild game, an extensive wine list, and dessert selections.
